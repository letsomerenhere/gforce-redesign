Success Stories.

Bringing focus, speed, execution and results when it matters most.

National Travel Center Operator

One of the largest U.S. operators of truck stops and travel centers partnered with GForce to modernize after more than 30 years on Lawson. By upgrading to Infor CloudSuite FSM, reengineering business processes, cleansing decades of data, and training over 200 users, GForce delivered one of the largest Lawson-to-FSM migrations in the industry.

The Result: a financial close cut from 20 days to 8, real-time reporting, seamless integrations, and full SOX compliance aligned with IPO readiness.

Learn more arrow_forward

SeaCube

SeaCube, one of the world’s largest purchasers and lessors of refrigerated containers, upgraded from Infor Lawson to CloudSuite FSM with GForce. The project modernized core processes, integrated a data lake, and eliminated reliance on outdated reporting tools. With strong user adoption from day one, SeaCube gained a modern, cloud-based platform that streamlined operations, improved reporting, and reduced the need for third-party applications.

Associated Grocers of Baton Rouge

GForce Technology is pleased to announce its selection as implementation partner for Associated Grocers of Baton Rouge's (AGBR) upgrade to Infor CloudSuite Financials & Supply Chain Management (FSM).

As a long-time Lawson/Infor user, AGBR sought a partner capable of harnessing innovative features while managing their legacy system transition and comprehensive data migration requirements.
