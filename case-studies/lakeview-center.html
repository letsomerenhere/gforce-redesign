Business Processes Streamlined and Successful Implementation Delivered

5 min read

Overview

Lakeview Center, part of Baptist Health Care, is a $150 million healthcare organization providing high-quality behavioral health, child protective, and vocational services. With over 2,000 employees, Lakeview sought to enhance customer service through more accurate data and improved communications. To achieve this, they decided to implement the 9.0 version of Infor Lawson Financials, Human Resources, Payroll, and Procurement.

A trusted partner with proven experience and expertise was needed to help them accomplish their goals, which included:

  • Eliminating or reducing the number of ancillary systems for Financial and Human Resources data
  • Automating and improving internal and external data exchanges
  • Economizing the implementation by leveraging current functionality

GForce Solution

GForce Technology Consulting provided project management, application, business process, and technical expertise throughout the lifecycle of the project. GForce's methodology, which prioritizes planning and design at the onset of the project, allowed Lakeview Center to take advantage of base functionality with minimal modifications, while streamlining their core business processes.

Benefits Delivered

Lakeview Center's Financial implementation resulted in improved visibility of cash disposition, spending, accounts receivable, and asset management. End users are now empowered to capture details directly in the sub-ledgers, simplifying the overall chart of accounts and enabling more consistent, timely, and relevant reporting.

Additional improvements came with the implementation of Lawson's Human Capital Management Suite, which strengthened security for sensitive employee data, provided better system controls, and automated employee benefit enrollment maintenance. Having more immediate access to information has enabled end users and employees to be less reliant on technical staff for data extraction, analysis, and ad hoc reporting.

Overall, the Lakeview Center implementation and subsequent project was deemed a success by all stakeholders. Business objectives were met by streamlining business processes, providing better visibility for decision-making, and enhancing employee customer service through more accurate data and improved communication.
