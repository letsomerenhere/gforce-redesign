Wayne HealthCare Goes Live!

GForce is excited to announce the successful completion of an Infor CloudSuite Financials and Supply Chain Management implementation for Wayne HealthCare. This comprehensive enterprise solution enhances their operational capabilities and positions Wayne HealthCare for continued growth in delivering exceptional patient care while streamlining their financial and supply chain management processes across their healthcare network.

4 min read

Overview

GForce is excited to announce the successful completion of an Infor CloudSuite Financials and Supply Chain Management implementation for Wayne HealthCare — a 52-bed hospital located in Greenville, Ohio. We are proud to have completed the project on time and under budget.

Infor was chosen over Workday and Oracle, and GForce was selected as the implementation partner to move Wayne HealthCare from CPSI to a modern cloud-based solution. Our team upheld GForce’s reputation as a reliable and efficient Certified Infor Alliance Partner, ensuring every stage of implementation aligned with client goals and readiness.

]

GForce Solution

GForce’s proven methodology emphasizes training throughout the implementation process, ensuring that Wayne HealthCare’s staff was ready to use their new system immediately upon go-live.

The project also included the implementation of the Mobile Supply Chain Module, which represented an enormous upgrade from their previous manual inventory counting process. Reporting is now more functional, data access is easier, and workflows have been streamlined, improving operational efficiency across departments.

Results at a Glance

  • Completed on time and under budget
  • Transitioned from CPSI to a modern, cloud-based solution
  • Implemented Infor CloudSuite Financials and Supply Chain Management
  • Introduced Mobile Supply Chain Module, eliminating manual inventory counts
  • Enhanced reporting and easier data access
  • Streamlined workflows and improved efficiency
  • Successful completion in just six months, despite limited internal resources

Project Highlights

When starting the project, it was clear that Wayne HealthCare had limited resources to devote to the implementation. Despite this, the GForce team worked tirelessly — both onsite and remotely — to ensure a smooth and efficient process.

The result was a successful outcome that not only met every project goal but also welcomed a new member into the GForce family. Wayne HealthCare now operates on a powerful, cloud-based system that supports scalability, accuracy, and operational excellence.
