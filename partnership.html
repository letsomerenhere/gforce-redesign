Who Is Infor?

Infor is a global leader in ERP, recognized for deep industry expertise and a cloud-first approach. With powerful solutions in Financials & Supply Management (FSM), Human Capital Management (HCM), and Workforce Management (WFM), Infor helps organizations simplify complexity, drive efficiency, and unlock new levels of performance and growth.
