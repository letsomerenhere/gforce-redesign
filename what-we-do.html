Infor Lawson to CloudSuite

Infor Lawson support sunsets in 2030, and organizations still relying on it face a strategic crossroads: wait and react, or move and modernize. At GForce, we guide clients through this transition with a team who have lived Lawson and championed CloudSuite.

Through focused business process discovery, we design and build solutions that harness CloudSuite’s full potential—tailored to your operations. We train your people as we go, so the end result is not just a powerful system, but one your functional teams truly own and can use to drive lasting results.

CloudSuite Implementation

Moving to Infor CloudSuite from an older ERP opens the door to faster processes, cleaner data, and sharper visibility. With GForce guiding the transition, you gain modern reporting and real-time insights that make decisions quicker and more informed. Daily operations run smoother, teams work with confidence, and the system adapts as the business evolves.

The result is more than new technology—it’s a platform that drives efficiency, supports growth, and equips your organization to compete at its best from day one.

Managed Services – CloudSuite

With GForce Managed Services, CloudSuite stays optimized, reliable, and aligned with your evolving business. We prevent problems before they disrupt, keep data and processes running flawlessly, and adapt the system to meet new demands.

And when it’s time for Phase 2 projects, we’re already in place and ready to expand functionality, unlock new capabilities, and keep your momentum moving forward.

Managed Services – Infor Lawson

Improve performance today, prepare for tomorrow. With GForce Managed Services, you get more from your current system while laying the groundwork for a smooth move to CloudSuite. We fine-tune processes, resolve issues before they disrupt, and keep data clean and reliable so when it’s time to migrate, you’re ready.

We can also help you adopt Landmark edge products that run in your Infor Lawson environment today and carry forward into CloudSuite so you step into the future with proven tools already in place.

Health Check

Whether you’re on Infor Lawson or CloudSuite, a GForce Health Check gives you a clear, actionable view of system performance and readiness. We uncover inefficiencies, risks, and opportunities to replace costly third-party tools with built-in Infor functionality and deploy AI to automate work, surface insights faster, and strengthen decision-making.

For Infor Lawson customers, it delivers quick wins now and a roadmap to CloudSuite. For CloudSuite users, it ensures you’re maximizing value, driving adoption, and planning for emerging capabilities. The result is a plan to eliminate waste, accelerate innovation, and unlock untapped potential.

PMO Services

GForce PMO Services give you the structure, oversight, and momentum to keep complex initiatives on track and delivering value. We align scope, schedule, and resources with your business goals, manage risks before they escalate, and ensure stakeholders stay informed and engaged.

Our team brings proven governance frameworks, real-world ERP experience, and the flexibility to adapt as priorities shift. The result is a disciplined, transparent approach that turns strategy into measurable outcomes—on time and on budget.

Change Management

GForce Change Management Services ensure your people are ready, confident, and equipped to thrive in new ways of working. We align leadership, engage stakeholders early, and deliver targeted communications and training that build adoption from day one.

Our approach blends proven methodology with deep ERP implementation experience, so change feels less like disruption and more like opportunity. The result is faster adoption, stronger user confidence, and lasting business results.
